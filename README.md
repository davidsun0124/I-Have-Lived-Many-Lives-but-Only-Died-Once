# 我活过很多次，但只死了一次

# I Have Lived Many Lives, but Only Died Once

<br>

> *在妈妈眼里，我是她的整个世界。在战报上，我是第三千九百九十七。*
>
> *穷人的孩子用命铺路，战争一停，西装革履的人踩着这条路走上谈判桌，握手、微笑、签字、分钱。*

<br>

> *In my mother's eyes, I was her entire world. On the casualty report, I was number 3,997.*
>
> *The children of the poor pave the road with their lives. When the war stops, men in tailored suits walk that road to the negotiating table — shaking hands, smiling, signing papers, dividing the spoils.*

---

## About / 关于

这是一篇反战叙事作品，以十四个视角讲述同一场战争：从婴儿到士兵，从无人机到母亲，从将军到商人。每一章都是一个"我"，每一个"我"都被战争碾过。

This is an anti-war narrative told through fourteen perspectives of the same war: from a baby to a soldier, from a drone to a mother, from a general to a businessman. Each chapter is an "I," and each "I" is crushed by war.
